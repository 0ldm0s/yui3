The TabView API will remain largely unchanged, with most attributes and methods having a 1:1 mapping from 2.x to 3.0.

tabView.addTab() tabView.addTab() no change

tabView.initAttributes() removed (handled by Widget) TBD

tabView.getTabIndex(tab) tabView.indexOf(tab) TabView.prototype.getTabIndex = TabView.prototype.indexOf
tabView.removeTab(2) tabView.removeTab(2) (inherit from List ?) no change
tabView.set/get('activeIndex') TBD (may be removed to avoid synch issues with activeTab) TBD

tabView.set/get('orientation') tabView.set/get('orientation') no change
